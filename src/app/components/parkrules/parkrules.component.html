White Rock Lake Dog Park Rules

All dogs must be at least 6 months old & up to date on vaccinations. (more) (less)

Puppies who have not had all three rounds of their puppy boosters are NOT protected against contagious viruses like PARVO, DISTEMPER, HEPATITIS and LEPTOSPIROSIS. They can also contract intestinal parasites if they are not on a Heartworm preventative with a parasite preventative element. Their little bodies cannot fight these infections if they are exposed to these illnesses and parasites.

No unsterilized dogs are allowed in either park. (more) (less)

ALL dogs must be sterilizied or have a special permit/tag for an intact dog. ALL dogs must be wearing tags including city registration.

There is a maximum of three dogs per person. (more) (less)

This mainly applies to dog walkers. If there is a problem there is no way one person can control the situation. BE SAFE.

Please do not bring food or treats into the park.

Always pick up after your pet. (more) (less)

There are dog bags available in all parts of the park.

Dallas City Dog Park Rules

No aggressive or dangerous dogs allowed.

No female dogs in heat are permitted in the park.

Please do not bring glass bottles into the park.

All pinch, prong or spiked collars must be removed.

Owners must fill in any hole dug by their dog(s).

Children under 12 must be strictly monitored.

No scooters, bikes, skateboards, etc.
